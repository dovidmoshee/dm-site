# Cohevo Visual Identity

## Logo usage
- Primary: wordmark "Cohevo"
- Keep clearspace equal to the cap-height of the C around the logo on all sides
- Do not distort, recolour, or place on a busy background

## Color palette

### Light mode
- Background: #f4f8ef
- Background alt: #e8efde
- Ink (primary text): #0f150f
- Ink 2 (secondary text): #2a3627
- Ink 3 (muted text): #586652
- Ink faint: #8a967f
- Accent (dark green): #4a7a00
- Accent fill (electric lime): #c8ff00
- On accent: #0f1a0b

### Dark mode
- Background: #0c130d
- Background alt: #131d14
- Ink (primary text): #edf6e8
- Ink 2: #c7d9be
- Ink 3: #96a58d
- Accent: #c8ff00
- Accent fill: #c8ff00

## Typography
- Primary sans: Instrument Sans (headlines and body)
- Accent serif: Instrument Serif (italic callouts and pull quotes)
- Monospace: DM Mono (labels, code, eyebrow text)

## UI style rules
- Rounded corners (12–20px)
- Generous whitespace
- Simple iconography — single-stroke lines
- Avoid dense text blocks
- Electric lime (#c8ff00) used sparingly as a focal accent

## Image direction
- Clean interfaces and workflow diagrams
- Real tool screenshots and automation flows
- Minimal visual clutter
- Execution and process moments over lifestyle shots
