# Cohevo Brand Foundation

## Brand name
Cohevo

## Tagline
Install the operating system your business runs on.

## Positioning statement
Cohevo helps SaaS startups and agencies with 3 to 20 people build the operating infrastructure they need to scale — mapped workflows, the right tools, automations that connect them, and AI where it actually saves time.

## Ideal clients
- SaaS startups with 3 to 20 people
- Agencies with 3 to 20 people
- Teams with existing revenue growing faster than their systems
- Founders who are the bottleneck in their own operations

## Core problems solved
- Processes living in people's heads — nothing is documented
- Manual data re-entry and copy-paste between disconnected tools
- Tool sprawl — many subscriptions with no clear owner or purpose
- Founder or ops lead stuck in every loop, unable to delegate

## Core promise
In 60 days, Cohevo maps, designs, builds, and hands off a complete operating infrastructure — documented workflows, the right tool stack, live automations, and AI where it genuinely saves time.

## Offer
Business OS Setup — 60-day structured engagement

## Four phases
1. Discover — Audit tools, workflows, and pain points. Deliver the Business Systems Map.
2. Design — Architect the tool stack and automation logic. Get client sign-off before building.
3. Implement — Build automations, AI workflows, and documentation. Test everything.
4. Train — Walk the team through the new system. Hand off cleanly.

## Brand values
- Clarity — every recommendation comes with a clear reason
- Simplicity — the best system is the one the team will actually use
- Reliability — automations that break are worse than no automations at all
- Documentation — if it is not documented, it does not exist

## Voice and tone
- Clear, direct, practical
- First-person singular ("I") — sole operator, not an agency
- Short sentences
- No jargon without definition
- Confident but not hype-driven
- Write like a person, not a marketing team
