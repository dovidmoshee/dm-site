# KPI Dashboard Template — Cohevo

## Weekly KPIs
- New leads
- Qualified leads
- Discovery calls booked
- Proposals sent
- Close rate
- Revenue closed
- Average deal size
- Delivery margin
- Client satisfaction score

## Monthly KPIs
- Monthly recurring revenue
- Total booked revenue
- Revenue by offer tier
- Churned clients
- Average project cycle time
- Team utilization

## Targets (example)
- Close rate: >= 25%
- Gross margin: >= 60%
- On-time delivery: >= 90%
- Client NPS: >= 8/10
