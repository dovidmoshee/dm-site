# Improved scoring notes

The first scoring pass was useful, but it overweighted generic founder titles and raw message count. Going forward, use this order:

1. Recent commercial intent
Example: Danny asking about AI business automation is more valuable than 100 old messages.

2. ICP/workflow fit
Prioritize companies where handoffs are inherently messy: agencies, SaaS, outsourcing, staffing, logistics, bookkeeping, education programs, production studios, ecommerce + projects, IT/implementation firms.

3. Buyer role
Founder/CEO matters, but COO, VP Ops, Head of Delivery, Customer Success, RevOps, Implementation, and Project/Program leaders may feel the pain more directly.

4. Warmth quality
Recent, relevant conversation beats old generic connection messages.

5. Disqualification risk
Suppress or downgrade enterprise employees, nonprofits with unclear budget, consumer/local businesses with low ops complexity, same-name collisions, and adjacent AI consultants unless framed as partner/referral.
