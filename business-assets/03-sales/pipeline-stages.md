# Cohevo Sales Pipeline

## Pipeline stages
1. New lead
2. Qualified
3. Discovery call booked
4. Discovery call completed
5. Proposal sent
6. Verbal yes
7. Contract signed
8. Paid and onboarded
9. Closed lost

## Exit criteria per stage
- Qualified: ICP fit confirmed and clear pain identified
- Discovery completed: goals, blockers, budget, timeline documented
- Proposal sent: scope, pricing, and timeline approved internally
- Contract signed: signed agreement and first payment received

## Required CRM fields
- Company name
- Contact name and role
- Lead source
- Pain summary
- Budget range
- Decision timeline
- Next action date
