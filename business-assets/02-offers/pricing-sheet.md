- Systems Stewardship Retainer: $2,500-$6,000/month

- 50% upfront to start
- 50% before final handoff

## What affects pricing
- Number of workflows in scope
- Number of tools and integrations
- Team size and stakeholder count
- Reporting complexity

## Discount policy
- Do not discount by default
- Use scoped trade-offs instead of price cuts
- If needed, cap discount at 10% for upfront full payment
