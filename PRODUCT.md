# Product: Cohevo Practical Technology Help

## The job of the site

Turn a person with a frustrating technology problem into a low-friction conversation with David.

The visitor does not need to understand the technical cause. The site should help them recognize that their problem belongs here, see a clear starting price, and send a sentence, screenshot, photo, or voice note.

## Audience

1. People anywhere who need remote help with software, backups, file transfers, email, cloud storage, device setup, and everyday technology problems.
2. Small-business owners anywhere who need help with websites, domains, business email, forms, files, tools, and everyday technology workflows.
3. Local clients in Carmei Gat and Kiryat Gat who need physical computer repair, upgrades, or onsite help.

## Positioning

**Friendly, practical technology help for people and small businesses.**

Lead with immediate, concrete technology rescue. Use the first job to reveal and earn the right to solve larger business-technology problems.

The primary promise is relief, not transformation:

> You do not need to diagnose it first. Show me what is happening and we will start there.

## Offers

### Quick Tech Rescue
- ₪450 remote
- ₪600 onsite
- Up to 90 minutes
- Parts, paid software, extensive travel, and work beyond the session are discussed before continuing

### Small Business Tech Cleanup
- Starting at ₪2,500
- Review computers, website, email, files, forms, backups, and workflow
- Select and fix the three most important problems in the agreed scope

### Monthly Tech Care
- ₪900 per month
- Two hours of remote support
- Priority response during working hours
- Basic website, domain, and backup checks
- Additional work quoted separately

## Conversion priorities

1. WhatsApp conversation
2. Short contact-form submission
3. Email

## Voice

Direct, capable, patient, plainspoken, and human.

Avoid consulting jargon, sweeping transformation promises, worldwide-agency positioning, wellness language, urgency tricks, and long explanations before the first call to action.

## Accessibility

WCAG AA baseline. Strong contrast, visible focus states, minimum 44px touch targets, readable line lengths, predictable navigation, reduced-motion support, and explicit form labels.
